5 things you need to know about Perry

family

Perry was named by her so busy owner when she was little, her mother Muslimah, lives few blocks from our house at Ilupeju.
Perry was adopted when she was little over a year ago. she got accepted into the family of Al azeem as everyone love to play with perry, except few like aboo Dawood and Aboo Zayd.
Right now, Perry has given birth to 9 beautiful kittens and she is growing older with more experience.

colour

The beautiful perry, just like a typical Nigerian cat has most of body covered with white fur, with some patches of brown and black around the back that extends to her head and her tail.
she has a yellowish brown eye laced with black like 'tiro'.

daily habit

Her daily habit is mainly three:

  • Play
  • Eat
  • Sleep
  • PES.

best food

Perry cries for any food that scent delicious, but she loves meat and fish, either cooked or raw.

secret life

Once in a while, she finds her way outside in the midnight through a secret path she created in the kitchen. The nights, she meets with friends, male cats, and sometimes, she hunts.

Do I love Perry

Of course, Perry can be annoying,she has been caught stealing from the pot, pooing in the passage and so on, also, she has helped hunt down the rats in the house, played with us when we lonely, and eaten our left over foods. overall, we've spent good times together and I love her, I love Perry. learn more about Perry
